Why Give

Your Gift Makes a Difference

By supporting the Likoudis Legacy Foundation, you honor James Likoudis' legacy and meaningfully contribute to the advancement of the faith in contemporary society. As a donor, you'll receive recognition and play a part in advancing theological scholarship, ecumenical and interfaith dialogue, and the betterment of society.
