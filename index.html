Our Mission

A Foundation for the
Long Work of Unity

The Likoudis Legacy Foundation is organized exclusively for charitable, educational, and scientific purposes under Section 501(c)(3). It carries forward the intellectual heritage of theologian James Likoudis — nearly a century of faith, scholarship, and devotion to Christian unity.

Established in his memory, the Foundation continues his advocacy for the Church's unity, the dignity of the human person, and the freedom of the faith to flourish in every corner of the world.

Learn about the Foundation →

I.

Ecumenical Dialogue

To foster ecumenical dialogue by building bridges across diverse Christian traditions in pursuit of greater Christian unity.

II.

Human Dignity & Peace

To uphold human dignity and peace by promoting principles that foster understanding, respect, and global harmony.

III.

Religious Liberty

To advance religious liberty by championing the rights of communities where the Christian faith is under threat.

Endorsements

Voices for the Legacy

I knew Dr. Likoudis for some twenty-five years… Dr. Likoudis' work as the author of numerous books and articles significantly contributed to clarifying and enhancing the relations between the Roman Catholic Church and the Eastern Orthodox Churches. He is regarded as a true Christian scholar.

Raymond Cardinal Burke

Prefect Emeritus of the Apostolic Signatura

James Likoudis was a courageous defender of the faith and a gentle 'man of the Church.' It is praiseworthy that this new Foundation has been established in his honor… May this initiative enrich the Church's pursuit of Christian unity.

Archbishop Joseph F. Naumann

Archbishop of Kansas City

I have known James Likoudis for half of my life and a third of his… He recognizes that conversion—any conversion—is not the matter of a moment. It is the love of a lifetime.

Scott Hahn, PhD

President, St. Paul Center for Biblical Theology

Likoudis occupies a singular place in the literature of his time. Faithful, yet not crazy. Very few people got it so right.

Mike Aquilina

Executive Vice President, St. Paul Center for Biblical Theology

Dr. Likoudis is a wonderful example of how to dialogue with the Orthodox about important differences without falling into a rigid fundamentalism that refuses to see grace present in the Orthodox church.

Michael Lofton

Founder, Reason and Theology

James Likoudis was one of several mentors from whom I was privileged to learn… Likoudis was already established as a proponent of authentic Catholic renewal, a leading opponent of sex education in schools, and a champion of the Magisterium.

Jeffrey Mirus, PhD

Founder, CatholicCulture.org

In the field of Catholic-Orthodox dialogue, James Likoudis figures as one of the most prominent Catholic apologists.

Jose Pereira, PhD

Professor Emeritus of Theology, Fordham University

In our modern era of mass communication, the Ecumenical work of reunion will not be carried out in full without lay involvement. And there is, perhaps, no member of the laity of either of the 'two lungs' of apostolic faith that has done more to further the dialogue than James Likoudis.

Tim Staples

Senior Apologist, Catholic Answers

For many years… James Likoudis has been a tireless laborer for fraternal dialogue between Catholic and Orthodox Christians. Few know these two ecclesial worlds so thoroughly.
