Research & Scholarship

Fellows Program

Supporting scholars who advance the work of James Likoudis

About the Program

Concentrated Research on a Living Tradition

The Likoudis Legacy Foundation is working towards offering a Fellows Program for scholars desiring to concentrate for 2 to 8 weeks on research and writing on a topic related to the work of James Likoudis.

Candidates who are admitted as Fellows are provided a stipend of between $1,000 to $2,000 depending on qualifications and scope of research.

The program is still under development, and more specific details are forthcoming. We welcome inquiries from qualified scholars in theology, philosophy, history, and related disciplines.

Scholarly Criteria

Who Should Apply

The following criteria will be taken into account when considering applications. The lack of any one criterion should not be considered a disqualification:

  • —MA thesis or Ph.D. dissertation, completed or in-progress
  • —Conference presentations
  • —College courses taught
  • —Peer-reviewed publications, including books and journal articles
Financial Support

Stipends & Grants

$1,000–2,000

Research Stipend

Provided as a lump sum for all accepted fellows, ranging based on qualifications and length of participation.

$500

Lodging Grant

Available to students who do not live within 50 miles of Washington, DC.

$500

Travel Grant

Available to students traveling from outside of North America.

How to Apply

Application Process

Applications are submitted through the Likoudis Legacy Application Portal (coming soon). The following documents are required:

Required Documents

  1. Complete application form
  2. Curriculum Vitae, including a complete list of publications
  3. Two letters of recommendation from professors or mentors
  4. Research proposal clearly detailing the topic of study and research plan
